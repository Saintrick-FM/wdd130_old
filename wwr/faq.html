Have any Question? We're here to assist you

Is this a good trip for kids?

Rafting can be a suitable trip for kids. Assess your child's experience and comfort level with water activities and adventure sports. If they have prior experience with water-based activities like swimming, they may be more likely to enjoy rafting..

Is whitewater rafting safe?

Whitewater rafting, like any adventure sport, carries inherent risks, but with our safety measures and precautions, trust us it is a safe activity. Commercial rafting outfitters prioritize safety and take several precautions to mitigate risks.

What is included in the trip?

The outfitter provides the necessary rafting equipment, including inflatable rafts, paddles, life jackets (personal flotation devices), and helmets. These items are essential for safety and are typically provided for each participant.

What do I need to bring?

Prepare a Swimwear, a quick-drying clothing, water Shoes or Secure Footwear, a sun Protection, a waterproof Bag, a towel, snacks and Energy Bars, and a water Bottle.

What is great about rafting anyway?

Rafting provides an adrenaline rush and a sense of adventure.Rafting is a physically demanding activity that engages various muscle groups in your body.

How far shold book my trip?

If you plan to go rafting during the peak season or a popular holiday period, it's advisable to book well in advance, about at least 3 months before you're intented day to come and raft.
